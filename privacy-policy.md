# Privacy Policy — Dynamics Kick Up

**Last updated: April 2026**

Dynamics Kick Up is developed by ProVision (Ossama Abdallah).

## Data Collection
This extension does not collect, store, transmit, or share any 
user data whatsoever.

## How It Works
All operations run entirely within your browser and your own 
authenticated Dynamics 365 / Power Apps session. No data is 
sent to any external server, including the developer's servers.

## What the Extension Accesses
The extension reads Dynamics 365 form fields and record data 
solely to display it back to you within the extension popup. 
This data never leaves your browser or your Dynamics 365 tenant.

## No Tracking
The extension contains no analytics, no telemetry, no crash 
reporting, and no usage tracking of any kind.
